Geeb Off

  • Learn how to geeb

    Regulation Size Geeb

    Find an empty plastic bottle. An empty water bottle or liter of soda will work. You can use any size bottle, but keep in mind that the bigger the bottle, the bigger the gravity bong hits will be, the more points you'll deserve.

    Starting a Match

    Make the geeb and then your ready to play, create an account and then queue up to be matched with a random player. The geeb off will begin once both teams are ready.

    The Standard Mode

    The standard game mode will consist of 5 rounds, each team must take a geeb within a minute of their opponment, whoever is the slowest loses the geeb off.

    Who can call Fouls

    The person with the higher ELO rating will be in charge of calling a foul. If the reports are too high for the day- the user will then recieve a penality.
